Dispose

The idea behind our process is simple; dispose of the correct trash into the correct bins to maximize the waste diversion rate for the city.

Our bins allow for all yard waste except:

  • Grass clippings
  • Rocks
  • Over sized branches

We also encourage you to seperate your compostable materials and put them in the bin as well. Some common items include:

  • Fruits and Vegetables
  • Coffee grounds and filters
  • Paper Bags
  • News Paper
  • Cotton Balls
  • Cereal

Bin Collection

You will pull your bin out to the curb just like the rest of the trash collection, and it will be collected on the same day. Certain months will have free additional collection of yard waste specifically as the volume increases. A schedule will be posted.

Decomposition

Once the waste is diverted, it will then be brought to a composting facility where it will begin the decomposition process. This process can take anywhere from 2 weeks up to 6 months depending on the items that are being broken down. We then plan on distributing the compost back into the community or local farms.
